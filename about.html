Our History

Founded in 1993 in Sofia, Furniture World has grown from a small 15 square meter store to the largest furniture store in Bulgaria, with several key milestones and expansions along the way.

Significant developments include the opening of our largest store in Sofia in 2006, the construction of a logistics center in 2008, marking a new era of growth and stability.
